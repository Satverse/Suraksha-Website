Suraksha Privacy Policy

At Suraksha, our utmost priority is your safety along with the protection of your personal data. This Privacy Policy elucidates how Suraksha collects, uses, shares, and safeguards your information. By using our app, you consent to the practices detailed herein.

1. Data We Collect
Personal Information: This encompasses details like your name, email address, phone number, age, address, pin code, city, and state.
Location Data: When the GPS is activated, your location coordinates are fetched for immediate response during SOS activations. If the GPS is off, we do not capture the location but notify your emergency contacts regarding its status.
Emergency Contacts: These details are stored both in your phone's memory and the third-party database "Appwrite." For emergency alerts, the app utilizes contacts from your phone memory.
2. How We Use Your Data
To enable the core features of Suraksha, such as sending SOS alerts and GPS status during emergencies.
To dispatch email notifications, reminders, updates, and newsletters pertinent to Suraksha.
For internal analysis and enhancement of app functionalities.

3. Data Sharing
Emergency Situations: In emergent situations, your location coordinates (if GPS is on) or GPS status (if off) are communicated to your emergency contacts.
Third-party Service Providers: We employ the third-party database "Appwrite" for saving emergency contact details. They access this specific data solely for this purpose and are contractually bound not to disclose or use it otherwise.

4. Data Security
We adopt best-in-class security protocols to shield your data from unauthorized intrusion, modification, or eradication.
Both Suraksha and "Appwrite" uphold stringent security standards to guarantee data protection.
Users are urged to maintain the confidentiality of their Suraksha account passwords.

5. Data Retention
We sustain your personal data only for the duration necessary to serve the purposes for which it was collected, inclusive of any legal, accounting, or reporting prerequisites.

6. Your Rights
Access: You possess the right to request a copy of the personal data we retain about you.
Correction: Upon identifying any inaccuracies in the data we hold about you, you have the right to ask for corrections.
Deletion: Under specific conditions, you can seek the deletion of your personal data

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy
Periodically, this Privacy Policy might undergo revisions. Such changes take effect immediately upon posting. It's advisable for users to frequently revisit this section to stay abreast of any updates.

8. Contact Us
For any uncertainties or concerns related to this Privacy Policy or the treatment of your data, feel free to connect with us at [Suraksha Support Email].

Conclusion
At Suraksha, your safety and privacy are paramount. We're resolute in our commitment to uphold your trust by being transparent about our data practices and safeguarding your data diligently. We encourage users to acquaint themselves with our privacy ethos, and for any concerns, we're here to assist.
Thank you for opting for Suraksha. Prioritize safety!
